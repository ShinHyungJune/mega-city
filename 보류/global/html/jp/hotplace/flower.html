Place-Flower Island

“I’ll let you walk the flower path”

you can see different flowers
in each flowering season

On the Paroho Flower Island

You can see rape flowers and azaleas in warm spring, poppies and roses in summer when the sun shines, and crape myrtle, cosmos, buckwheat flowers, and sunflowers in contrast to the sky blue in autumn when the sky is blue. And in early winter, silver grass is spreading on the road. It is a place that welcomes us on a flower path no matter what season we visit.
At the entrance to the flower island, you can see cute rabbits. These rabbits, which act as mascots for the flower island, are popular with local residents and children. Once you see these rabbits, you can't just overlook them, so anyone will stop and be fascinated by the cuteness of these rabbits. After greeting the rabbits, let's take a tour of the flower island. Since the flower island itself is quite large, it is recommended to take time and take a leisurely walk around.

Flower island can be divided into metasequoia road, evergreen garden, seasonal garden,
iris garden, and central garden. There is also a pond next to the Evergreen Garden.

One island is decorated as if it were a botanical garden. It is recommended to visit between spring and summer
to see the green grasses and flowers of various colors. It is better to walk the entire island and slowly explore
all the places rather than dividing the sections. If you feel the comfort and coolness in Metasequoia Road, you can
feel the warmth while looking at the colorful flowers in the flower garden.

Looking at the entire island, it feels like being surrounded by green grass, but the flowers in full bloom between the mountains and the trees create beauty.

Various flowers of red, purple, yellow, and white are spread out, boasting their own colors. As I walk along the flowers and the wind, I feel like I want to pause and appreciate.

The biggest attraction of Flower Island is the pavilions and benches scattered throughout. There is a space where you can sit where you want to relax, as if you were reading the hearts of people walking through the flower island. You can take a leisurely look around while taking a walk, and sit for a while when your legs hurt, and you can take a moment to see the flowers in motion.

If you choose a spot you like and take the time to look at the landscape you want to see, a piece of memory is completed with just the sound of the wind and the scent of flowers.

The complete rest of
the flower island

Paroho Flower Island is included in the Yanggu 10-year Jangsaeng-gil course, and is usually loved by locals for exercise and walking. If you walk this path, you can feel the energy that will really make you young like a flower. As there is a saying that if you look at things that are pretty and beautiful, they resemble them, and the flower island and Yanggu give us fresh energy with beautiful scenery.

Beautiful trees and flowers on the tranquil Lake Paro, the fresh air of Yanggu and the blue sky are united as a flower island. It's okay if you don't have to look for pretty flowers. Just walking along the beautifully unfolded road reveals a picturesque landscape wherever you can see.

The flower island is a space that gives many people a rest as a space itself. Taking a break while breathing fresh air is something everyone dreams of, but not everyone can do. These days it is difficult for many people to breathe in a stuffy space and see the open sky. That is why the rest of the flower island is even more special. Beyond the city surrounded by concrete, all you can see in front of you is a green forest, a blue sky, and the clouds floating above it, spreading out in front of you as if you could grab it.

I walk along the road and stop in a pretty place, take the scenery in my eyes for a moment, and walk again along the flowers. If you want, stop at the place you like the most among the relaxation spaces located everywhere and pass time again.

Instead of chasing the landscape to see something like that, if I walk as I go, neither the sky nor the trees nor the forest are all there even if I do not follow. No matter where you look at it, it's beautiful in different light from season to season, and you don't go anywhere and always show off your beautiful figure on the spot. We just have to enjoy them. It's a very brief moment, but just stopping here and taking a deep breath can help us feel that we're already out of the stuffy city.

We always follow the beautiful scenery to find a place to rest for relaxation, and differentiate between time to visit and time to rest. It is not a place for relaxation, but a place where time and space itself presents a complete rest, a place that shows us pretty flowers with different charms for each season. It gives us the strength to take a break from walking and walk again.

Many people are afraid to do nothing. I think I have to see something and I have to do something, but I don't have a good time. It's important to see and experience so many things, but in order for us to go long and far, we need a “full break” for ourselves. It is “Flower Island” where you can get energy through relaxation in the clean air and nature provided by Yanggu.
