Punch bowl

It is a basin developed at an altitude of 400-500m
on the coast of Yanggu-gun, and is located about
22km northeast of Yanggu-gun.

Korean Peninsula Island

Yanggu Korean Peninsula Island is
the largest artificial wetland in Korea
located upstream of Lake Faro

Flower island

On the Paroho Flower Island,
you can see different flowers in each flowering season.

Yanggu nature Eco Park

Yanggu Arboretum is registered as
the 6th public arboretum in the province,
and you can enjoy over 1000 kinds of trees and plants in one place.

Park Soo Keun Museum

Park Soo-geun is a painter who devoted his life to drawing
“human goodness and truthfulness” based on the life
of an unnamed and poor commoner.

Yanggu Porcelain Museum

At the White Porcelain Museum,
which shows the 600-year history of white porcelain in Yanggu,
you can appreciate the lofty shape of Joseon white porcelain produced
by Yanggu white clay, centering on the blue and white porcelain,
the last flower of Joseon porcelain.

Center of Korea Observatory

The opportunity to see the wide sky in the center
of the Korean Peninsula will be a fun experience and a special memory.

Park Soo-geun Museum of Art

The Park Soo-geun Museum of Art possesses a number of artifacts, oil paintings, watercolors, drawings, prints, illustrations, etc., and repairs and permanent displays. In addition, the museum holds a variety of works of major contemporary Korean painting companies that were active in the same era as Park Soo-geun and plans and exhibits them.
