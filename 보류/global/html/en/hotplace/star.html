“The sky seen from the center of the country”

Since when did Yanggu become
the center of the Korean Peninsula

Is it really right to be in the middle of the Korean Peninsula?” When I travel to Yanggu on the map, I am curious to see the location of Yanggu on the map. Considering that the concept of Korean territory based on the Constitution of the Republic of Korea is the Korean Peninsula and its annexed islands, it is possible to establish Korea's official four poles, including the islands. Based on this 4 pole point, the intersection of the central meridian and the hypocrisy is the center point of Korea. The coordinates of the central point of the Korean Peninsula are 128 ˚02’02.5 ˚ east longitude and 38 ˚03’37.5 ˚ north latitude. The area corresponding to this coordinate is the area of San 48 Dochon-ri, Nam-myeon, Yanggu-gun, Gangwon-do.

Based on this fact, Yanggu became the center of the country. The creation of the Yanggu National Land Administration Central Astronomical Observatory was opened in 2007 by constructing an astronomical observatory near the center of the Korean peninsula to allow you to see the sky from the center of Korea. The opportunity to see the wide sky from the center of the Korean Peninsula will be an interesting experience and a special memory.

Seeing the stars at the National Land Administration Central Observatory is more than simply looking at the sky and appreciating the stars. As I watched the stars embroidered in the black sky, the first thing I thought was why I lived without time to look at the sky. I thought about when I felt that the stars were beautiful as I looked at the sky and when I was wondering and wishing while looking at the stars, but those times were already a long time ago. It seems that it made it impossible for everyone to start counting stars while raising their heads and looking up at the sky.

Anyone who sees the stars of different colors embroidered in the dark night sky will feel beautiful and look at them with wonders. Seeing the stars in the sky is not only exciting and exciting for children, it is also very special for adults who are not often seen in cities and have no time to see. In particular, the stars shining in the sky at times when everyone is powerless like these days can give us great comfort just by their existence.

If seeing the stars at an observatory feels a bit overwhelming and professional, don't worry. Everyone has romance and curiosity about the universe, but will lack detailed knowledge of planets and stars. Even if you don't have to know exactly the planets and constellations, it's enough to get drunk on their beauty. However, if you want to learn, appreciate, and study constellations and planets more professionally, we recommend visiting Yanggu Guktojeong Central Observatory. In addition to observing the stars directly using the exhibition commentary program within the observatory, it is also possible to view the observatory and listen to detailed explanations.

If you've got your knowledge of the stars in the observatory,
let's go outside and face the sky directly.

There is a camping site in the National Land Jung Central Astronomical Observatory, and it is a romantic time just thinking of the starry sky you see while camping. It's a good way to watch indoors comfortably, but what's the perfect way to look up at the night sky is to breathe in the refreshing night air at the stars embedded in the infinite black sky looking up in complete darkness. It will be a gift-like time for both children who do not have the opportunity to see the fresh air and the clear sky, and adults who are enduring the frustrating daily life. Camping has become a hobby for people these days.

It is a time to escape from daily life for a while and to enjoy nature, and to escape from a city crowded with people and cars. Even if you don't have great equipment and skills, it's a perfect break to take a chair and a tent, settle in a nice mountain and take in the scenery. It can be annoying to have to pack your gear and have to eat, but camping is the only exception. It's not a restaurant with great service, and it's not a comfortable and comfortable hotel bed, but now people want to get away from the crowded and noisy places and go to their own healing spot, cook themselves even if they're clumsy, and sleep in a tent even if they're uncomfortable.

It's probably because nature returns all these inconveniences. So is one night at the observatory. If you have fully enjoyed the blue mountains and blue sky during the bright day when the sun is shining, if you lie comfortably in the dark darkness and look up at the sky, the sky seems to shine beautifully, giving us comfort. If you look at the sparkling stars like that, you will be able to see what kind of star it is, and the shape of a constellation will appear, and your curiosity will rise. It can be said that the biggest attraction of the astronomical observatory camping site is to look for what you see and hear with your eyes at night. It is one of the fun to see the stars that each person feels different even when they see the same scenery. It is said that even if you see the same star, it is different if you look for a constellation directly after the sun goes down after getting information about the constellations and planets while viewing the observatory. In this case, if you make a small bet, your memories at the campsite will be a little more perfect.

The stars in the night sky viewed from the observatory cannot be expressed in words or words. What is so regrettable while writing is that it is not able to perfectly express the beauty seen with my own eyes. This place presents a time of seeing the stars in the sky in the dark late at night, the only stars that I can think of while breathing in the cool, fresh air.

When it comes to “night view,” we would be accustomed to going up high and watching the colorful lights unfolding below. However, if you want to see a real'night view', you don't want to climb up high and look at colorful and colorful lights. Instead, step on the ground under your feet and look up at the sky and see the stars shining from different places in the black sky. The night sky captured with both eyes from the center of the Korean peninsula will, I bet, fill everyone with dreams and memories that shine like stars.
