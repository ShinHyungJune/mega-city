Yanggu Arboretum

“Healing trip along the beautiful trail in the forest”

That the whole family
is satisfied with

The five senses

Yanggu Arboretum is registered as the 6th public arboretum in the province, and you can enjoy over 1000 kinds of trees and plants in one place. Yanggu Arboretum, located at the foot of Daeamsan Mountain in Yanggu-gun, started with the creation of an ecological botanical garden in 2004, and it can be said to be a nature-centered arboretum that combines DMZ Wild Animal Ecology Center, DMZ Wild Flower Garden, Wood Culture Experience Center, and DMZ barrier-free sharing road and ecological trail. It is a place where you can feel the natural ecology with your five senses at 450m above sea level where the natural ecology is well preserved.

Yanggu Arboretum is divided into three themed areas. There is a “forest raising ground”
where you can meet the ecology of the forest and various flowers and trees, a “forest playground”
where children can play freely and a performance hall, and a “forest learning ground”
where you can see flowers by feature.

In the Forest Growing Site, you can see flowers by various themes, such as flowers with interesting stories such as preservation of endangered plants, breeding facilities, glass greenhouses, and story flowers, and plants at risk of extinction. Looking at endangered plants can help you think about how important it is to preserve ecosystems. In the greenhouse located here, you can enjoy ground cover plants harmonized with various domestic bonsai trees. In addition, you can enjoy the flowers while browsing the stories related to flowers, such as legends passed down from ancient times and the stories by which names were given.

Forest playgrounds are located throughout the botanical garden, and as you walk inside, you can often see spaces for children. There is also a shallow valley for children to enjoy, a playground that expresses the universe, and a large garden in the shade of zelkova trees where families can enjoy the feeling of having a picnic together. The playground with the concept of space is a space where children can enjoy and develop their imagination by installing photo zones and constellations such as the moon and spaceships that represent alien characters and solar systems that children like.

For children who are bored with just walking, this is a place where children can play freely by stopping by the playground. If you've been running hard in space, it's time to cool off your sweat for a while. In the picnic plaza decorated with mushroom sculptures, the Daeamsan valley water flows shallowly and is decorated so that children and adults can enjoy and play safely. Finally, in the pond fountain and open-air theater decorated with old trees and insects, you feel as if you are in a fairy tale. You can also throw coins into the pond to make good luck memories. It is a space where all families with children can enjoy and enjoy.

Forest Learning Center is a place where you can learn about the forest while looking at the ecology of Daeamsan Mountain. Wildflowers that grow naturally in Mt. Daeam were planted to minimize human power and create an ecological space for self-breeding. Here, you can learn by closely observing the life of wildflowers, from the appearance of planting and bud growth to fruiting. It is a great place to learn while watching closely that plants are born, grow, and reproduce like animals. It will be a great experience for adults as well as children to feel the preciousness and mystery of life.

Let's get out of the wildflower garden and walk to a little romantic place this time. The romantic garden decorated with colorful flowers, rich scented gujeolcho, and thyme is good to get drunk with the scent of flowers while listening to the sound of the flowing water. The delicate floral scent makes the surrounding air beautiful. You can walk around the arboretum as if you were in your own garden, admiring the mysterious flowers such as hepatica, Geumgang lanterns, and wind flowers that bloom differently from season to season along the promenade in the forest. When walking on a floating deck path, you will also be given the opportunity to see wetland plants more closely.

Throughout the botanical garden, you can feel that the only thing that forests give to humans is good. When I breathe in the phytoncide emanating from the pine tree as much as I can, the bad energy in the body escapes, and only the scent of the forest fills me.

A place where both the body and the mind become refreshed Is there any other time as happy as walking with a loved one?

Listen to the story of the forest

In the arboretum, there is a walking path that connects the Wild Animal Ecology Center and the Bonsai Garden, and wooden bridges and deck shelters have been built so that visitors do not get bored while walking lightly. Information boards and photo zone signs for each walking course are well installed throughout, so even without a commentator, it is possible to fully explore and understand the arboretum.

However, if you want to enjoy the Yanggu Arboretum a little deeper, I recommend using the forest commentary operated by the Arboretum. In front of the forest commentator's office located in the arboretum, a dense hat is also provided to avoid the sunlight. Walking on a forest path wearing a dense hat reminds me of a time when I was a child, gathering insects and playing in the mountains. As I walk along the natural forest path to revive memories, I wonder what kind of trees are planted on the path I am walking and what insects are there. Forest commentators who help me to solve those questions walk together to deepen the arboretum. It will help you feel.

If you listen to the explanation as if you are walking and talking together rather than a difficult and grand explanation, you can experience the surrounding scenery that looked like just green plants and colorful flowers more interesting and mysterious than before. The experience of seeing the forest up close and listening to the story of the forest is of great help in broadening your understanding of nature.

The forest commentary is open from 10:00 to 15:00 in winter and 10:00 to 16:30 in summer. It is recommended to visit the arboretum before opening hours and use the description according to time. It will be a time to feel that interesting study is just like this. I think it is a good memory for not only children full of curiosity, but also adults who do not have specialized knowledge, although they encounter nature.
